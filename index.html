Welcome to Kernal Krazy

Thanks for "popping" in!

Kernal Krazy Popcorn has returned to Southeast Nebraska. YAY! The popcorn comes in Regular, Cheese, Cheesy Jalapeno, Kettle, and Karamel flavors that are avaliable year round. Seasonal flavors we offer are Autumn Delight, Karmel Apple, Strawberry Cream, Karmel Delight, Muddy Kettle along with more!

Also, Husker fans must try our special combination of cinnamon and vanilla popcorn, Go Big Red!

Our factory is located in Tecumseh, Nebraska and is owned and operated by Region V Services in Auburn, NE.
