Our Story - "Meet the Team"

Chad (left) has been employed at the popcorn factory for three years. His favorite part of working at the popcorn factory is the smell - he loves the smell of popcorn.

Beth (middle) has been employed for two years. Her favorite part of the popcorn factory is bagging the popcorn once it has dried.

Carl (right) has been employed at the factory for the last eight years. He is the head popper and that is also what his favorite part about the popcorn factory is.

Kernal Krazy is owned and operated by Region V Services, in Southeast Nebraska. They employ and provide services for people with developmental disabilities. Region V has been in existence for 38 years and purchased Kernal Krazy approximately eight years ago. Kernal Krazy had been in operation for over thirty years.
